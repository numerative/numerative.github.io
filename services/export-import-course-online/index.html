Complete Export-Import Training Program

Master international trade with our comprehensive 12-module curriculum designed for practical success.

Module 1: Foundations of Global Trade

🔰 Build your foundation in international trade with comprehensive understanding of global trade systems, India's export potential, and key regulatory frameworks.

• Introduction to Export-Import: Scope, Legal Framework, and Business Models
• Global Trade Ecosystem: WTO, ICC, IMF, ITC, and Major Trade Blocs
• India's Export Legacy: Key Products, Markets & "Make in India" Vision
• Exporter & Importer Types: Roles, Responsibilities, and Legal Identity
• Foreign Trade Policy (FTP 2020–25): Structure, Benefits & Strategic Importance

Module 2: Business Setup & Legal Compliance

🧭 Navigate the legal landscape of international trade by establishing your business entity and obtaining all necessary registrations and certifications.

• Company Formation: Proprietorship, LLP, Pvt Ltd, Ltd
• Registration Essentials: PAN, GST, IEC, RCMC, AD Code
• Bank Account Setup & Currency Management (EEFC, Forex)
• Licenses & Certifications: FSSAI, ISO, FDA, MSME, Pollution Board
• Digital Presence: Website, Domain, Email, Social Media Setup

Module 3: Logistics, Shipping & Packaging

🚢 Master the logistics chain from packaging to delivery, understanding international shipping terms, container types, and freight forwarding processes.

• Incoterms® 2020: Costing, Risk, Delivery Terms
• Container Types: FCL, LCL, Reefer, Flat Rack, Tank
• Freight Forwarding: Role of CHA, Shipping Line, Airline
• Export Packing Standards: Labeling, Symbols, Sampling
• Practical: Freight Calculation & Booking Simulation

Module 4: Payment Terms & Export Finance

💰 Secure your transactions and manage financial risks with comprehensive knowledge of payment methods, trade finance, and forex management.

• Payment Modes: Advance, D/P, D/A, LC, BG
• Letter of Credit: UCP 600, Types, Flowchart
• Pre & Post Shipment Finance: EPC, PCFC, FCTL
• Forex Risk Management: Forward Contracts, EEFC, FEMA
• Practical: Finance Application & LC Drafting

Module 5: Product & Market Selection

📦 Strategically choose your export products and target markets using data-driven research, competitive analysis, and market intelligence.

• Strategic Product Selection: RCA, SWOT, Budget-Friendly Options
• Market Research Techniques: Market Mapping, Embassy Data, B2B Portals
• Competitor Analysis: Global Standards, Pricing, Demand Forecasting
• Trade Fairs & Exhibitions: Leveraging Events for Buyer Discovery
• Practical: Create Your Export Product Portfolio

Module 6: International Marketing & Buyer Engagement

📡 Develop powerful marketing strategies and build meaningful relationships with international buyers through digital platforms and professional communication.

• Branding & Positioning: Logo, Brochure, Stationery, Social Media
• B2B & B2C Strategies: LinkedIn, Alibaba, Indiamart, TradeIndia
• Buyer Communication: Email Formats, Negotiation Skills, Verification
• Proforma Invoice & Purchase Order
• Practical: Live Buyer Search & Outreach Simulation

Module 7: Buyer Finding Strategies & International Communication

🎤 Master the art of finding and connecting with genuine international buyers using proven search techniques, verification methods, and professional communication skills.

• Professional Email & Call Scripts
• Brand Creation: Name, Logo, Slogan, Registration
• Global Buyer Communication: Language Tools & Etiquette
• 10+ Proven Methods to Discover Genuine Buyers
• Free, Low-Cost & Premium Buyer Databases
• B2B Portals: LinkedIn, Alibaba, Indiamart, TradeIndia
• Trade Fairs, Exhibitions & Embassy Support
• Google Search Techniques & Paid Data Tools
• Buyer Verification: Domain Age, Credit Rating, Import History
• How Buyers Search for Exporters & How Exporters Should Search for Buyers
• Offline Buyer Discovery: Personal Visits, Referrals, Agents
• Social Media Buyer Outreach: Facebook, LinkedIn, YouTube
• Email Marketing Tools & Automation for Lead Generation
• Practical: Brand Pitch & Buyer Presentation Simulation

Module 8: Export Costing & Pricing Strategy

🧮 Develop competitive pricing strategies and accurate cost calculations to maximize profitability while remaining competitive in international markets.

• Export Pricing Worksheet: Cost Elements, Negotiation
• Quotation Preparation: Custom House Agent & Freight Forwarder
• Budget Export Models: Low-Cost Product Strategies
• Practical: Costing Simulation & Quotation Drafting

Module 9: Documentation & Customs Clearance

📑 Navigate the complex world of export documentation and customs procedures with hands-on training in preparing, filing, and managing all required paperwork.

• Pre-Shipment Documents: Invoice, Packing List, Certificates
• Post-Shipment Documents: BL, AWB, COO, SDF, GSP
• Customs Process: ICEGATE, EDI, Port Stuffing, LEO
• Inspection & Quality Control: EIC, Phytosanitary, Fumigation
• Practical: Document Preparation & Filing (Manual + Digital)

Module 10: Insurance & Risk Management

🛡️ Protect your business and shipments with comprehensive risk management strategies, insurance coverage, and dispute resolution mechanisms.

• ECGC: Role, Policies, Claim Process
• Marine Insurance: Cargo Clauses, Strike & War Risks
• Currency, Goods & Buyer Risk Mitigation
• Dispute Resolution & Recovery Agencies
• Practical: Risk Assessment & Insurance Filing

Module 11: Import Procedures & Documentation

🧳 Understand the complete import process from supplier selection to customs clearance, including documentation, duty calculations, and compliance requirements.

• Import Cycle: Licensing, Duty Calculation, Customs Clearance
• Import Documentation: Invoice, BOE, COO, Insurance
• High Sea Sales & Bonded Warehousing
• Import Pricing & Supplier Verification
• Practical: Import Order Execution & Duty Payment

Module 12: Government Schemes & Incentives

🏛️ Maximize your export potential by leveraging government schemes, incentives, and support programs designed to boost India's export competitiveness.

• MEIS, SEIS, RoDTEP, ROSCTL, MAI, MDA, MOOWR
• Advance Authorization, DFIA, EPCG
• Status Holder Recognition & Export Promotion Councils
• Practical: Claim Filing & Incentive Tracking

About the Founder

Welcome to Yashraj Exim Academy, your premier destination for comprehensive export-import training.

Led by Yash Chanchal, a seasoned professional with over 16 years of experience in international trade, shipping, logistics, freight forwarding, and international marketing, our academy is dedicated to empowering individuals and businesses to expand their global reach. Yash holds a Post Graduate Diploma in Export Management and International Business, and his expertise encompasses international marketing, freight forwarding, shipping and logistics, export documentation, and liaison coordination. At Yashraj Exim Academy, we provide in-depth knowledge and practical insights to help you navigate the complexities of the global market. Join us to embark on a journey towards international business success.
